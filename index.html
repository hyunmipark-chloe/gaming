Game Info News
Play as G-Ma and protect your secret recipe book from waves of alien chefs by planting crops and building vegetable themed turrets in this 3rd-person tower defense game!

Story

A group of alien chefs invade a small Earth farmhouse at the behest of their manager in an attempt to steal the owner’s cookbook, because they aren’t great at cooking and need the book to teach them the skills that are inside for use in their own cuisine. The owners of the farmhouse, labeled G-Ma and G-Pa by the aliens, must defend their home. With G-Pa on lookout and G-Ma out in the fields they work as a team to defeat the incoming alien waves with the help of crop turrets created from vegetables in G-Ma’s gardens.

Characters

G-Ma

A retired professional chef, G-Ma lives as a farmer with her husband G-Pa. Her favourite pastime is growing vegetables which she waters using her back equipped power washer, a tool for watering her crops that had been modified by G-Pa to make her life easier.

G-Pa

Lifelong farmer and G-Ma’s husband, G-Pa is an alien fanatic and master tinkerer. In his free time G-Pa pilots drones and hunts for proof of alien life. He also designs defensive turrets to protect the house if aliens ever invade.

Flying Dish Washer

Timid and nervous, they hide in their pots when faced with a challenge. Their special flying saucer allows them to avoid any gas or flames they might come into contact with.

Line Worker

The standard chef found on board the galactic space restaurant, and a loyal employee to the Manager. Some workers are equipped with oven mitts to withstand any heat on the job.

Head Chef

Created to be an incredibly large size, the Head Chef takes on a leadership role in the alien’s restaurant. Their tough skin allows them to ignore small nicks and cuts they might receive, it takes a lot to bring them down.

The Manager

The founder of the space faring restaurant, through their age and status they command the staff with dignity and pride. They have spent their entire life searching and gaining new cooking techniques, in an attempt to elevate the restaurant to new heights.

Space Chefs from Space a third-person tower defense game with farming elements. Play as G-Ma and protect your secret recipe book from waves of alien chefs by planting crops and building vegetable themed turrets!
